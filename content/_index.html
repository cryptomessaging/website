Privacy Isn't A Luxury

Privacy is an essential ingredient of all free societies

Cryptomessaging.org is creating open source software to allow free people to communicate privately. Services such as Signal already provide end-to-end encryption, but the centralized servers provide a chokepoint and the requirement that you use your phone number exposes too much personal information.

Cryptomessaging uses decentralized identity and messaging reflectors to create a truly private and durable infrastructure to exchange messages.
